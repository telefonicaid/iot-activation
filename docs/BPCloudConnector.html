What is Cloud Connector?

Cloud Connector is a service that publish on different public cloud the SIM and connectivity information available on the Kite Platform as your device does. It allows you to manage the information of your SIM‚Äôs as a resource from your devices With just a configuration change, network, consumption and location data can be sent to the Cloud

How to get it

Contact your Kite Provider and ask for it. Very soon you will be able to activate this service from your Kite platform.

If you already have the SIM cloud monitoring service available, you will have the option to activate the service for all SIM cards you wants, by including them in a Monitoring Group and activating Cloud Monitoring (ON mode).
